Through the Grimy Glass

Through the Grimy Glass is a dark experimental short film that explores connections between people through themes of disengagement and lonliness. The story will follow the relationship between the unusual tenants of a seedy apartment building and the creature living in the alley outside that is made of the trash they throw away. The film has just entered production.
