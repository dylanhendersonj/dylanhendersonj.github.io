Plastic Flowers Never Wilt

Plastic Flowers Never Wilt is a single-scene short animation meant to be a proof of concept to a longer short film idea. The scene observes a doctor perform an absurd and gruesome medical procedure intended to reverse the effects of aging. This film served as my first foray into miniature set building. The unedited footage was lost when the hard-drive containing it was stolen.
