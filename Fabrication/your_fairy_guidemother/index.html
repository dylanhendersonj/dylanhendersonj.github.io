Your Fairy Guidemother

Your Fairy Guidemother is a prospective educational children's show created by Rachel McIntosh of Razor Productions. The show would combine a live-action host with puppetry, stop-motion animation, and miniature sets. I had the pleasure of helping fabricate the world until the beginning of the Covid-19 pandemic.
