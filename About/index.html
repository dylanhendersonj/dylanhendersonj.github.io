Dylan is a Brooklyn based multimedia filmmaker. He uses the processes of stop-motion animation and narrative fictional storytelling to explore and engage with his areas of interest in an attempt to better understand them and reify them into film experiences people can share. His recent film work is linked by themes of personality formation. He also has experience as a cinematographer, his concentration of study while earning his B.A.

Dylan attended Emerson College in Boston and graduated with a B.A. in Film Production.
